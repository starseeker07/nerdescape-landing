How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Send a photo of your outfit

Take a photo or pick one from your gallery. Set the TPO (occasion) for your look and you're ready to go.

2

Seah analyzes your outfit with AI

Powered by Gemini AI, Seah evaluates your TPO match, body type, color coordination, and fit. Expect her signature brutally honest score and feedback.

3

Get feedback and shopping links

Receive specific improvement tips with curated shopping links. Use a paid ticket and score below 3.0, and you'll also get an AI-edited version of your outfit.
